Ube Roll

Larz suprises us all with his talent for baking. This is actually quite a hard recipe to pull off so be extra sure to watch the original video with the recipie linked below.

Here is the original recipe for Ube Roll created by the Babish Culinary Universe over on youtube.

Ingredients

Cake Ingredients

  • 3Tbsp Powdered Ube
  • 1/2C Water
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1/2C Granulated Sugar
  • 40g Cake Flour
  • 1/2tsp Ube Flavoring
  • 1/4tsp Violet Food Coloring
  • 2Tbsp Vegetable Oil

Buttercream Icing Ingredients

  • 6.5oz Egg Whites
  • 1C Sugar
  • 2tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 4 Stickes Butter, Softened
