Bagel Sandwiches

Originally from the show Steven Universe these bagel sandwiches are the perfect thing to distract some crystal critters, or snack on durring the long boat ride back home. The original creator of this recipe and myself both think that the best bagel is one made at home with this recipe. That being said feel free to still make the sandwich with storebought bagels if you need too.
