Bagel Sandwiches

Originally from the show Steven Universe these bagel sandwiches are the perfect thing to distract some crystal critters, or snack on durring the long boat ride back home. The original creator of this recipe and myself both think that the best bagel is one made at home with this recipe. That being said feel free to still make the sandwich with storebought bagels if you need too.

Here is the original recipe for Bagel Sandwiches created by the Babish Culinary Universe over on youtube.

Ingredients

Bagels

  • 650g Bread Flour
  • 1tsp Active Dry Yeast
  • 350g Water
  • 25g Granulated Sugar
  • 25g Diastatic Malt Powder (Can substitute with more bread flour)
  • 10g Kosher Salt
  • 24C Boiling Water
  • 1Tbsp Barley Syrup
  • 2tsp Baking Soda

Fried Egg & Turky Sandwich

  • 2 Slices of Turkey
  • 1 Egg
  • Bagel

Canadian Bacon & Egg Sandwich

  • 1-2 Peices of Canadian Bacon
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Slice Cheese
  • Bagel

Lox & Cream Cheese Sandwich

  • Cold Smoked Salmon
  • 1 Egg
  • Cream Cheese
  • Bagel
