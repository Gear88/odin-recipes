Cookie Cat

Originally from the show Steven Universe Cookie cats are a brand of icecream sandwich. When steven finds out that the product is being discontinued he gets rather upset and through that turmoil finds out that he got some of his mothers powers. While these sandwiches wont give you mistical powers they are pretty tasty.

Here is the original recipe for Cookie cat created by the Babish Culinary Universe over on youtube.

Ingredients

Cookie Ingredients

  • 160g Flour
  • 80g Cocoa Powder
  • 75g Butter, Soft
  • 50g Light Brown Sugar
  • 100g white sugar
  • 1/2tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/2tsp Salt
  • 3/4tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 100g Milk

No-Churn Vanilla Ice Cream Ingredients

  • 8oz Cold Heavy Cream
  • 2oz Clotted Cream (Substitute: Combine 1 oz mascarpone and 2 oz heavy cream)
  • 6oz Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 1/2tsp Salt
  • 1tsp Vanilla Extract

No-Churn Strawberry Ice Cream Ingredients

  • 8oz Strawberries
  • 6oz Heavy Cream
  • 2oz Clotted Cream (Substitute: Combine 1 oz mascarpone and 2 oz heavy cream)
  • 6oz Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 1/2tsp Salt
  • 1tsp Vanilla Extract
