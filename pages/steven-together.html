Together Breakfast

Steven really wanted to have breakfast with the gems but due to some shanagans the breakfast he spent a lot of time making got posesed and tried to eat all of them instead. This recipe is mostly garenteeded to not come alive and try to eat you. (This statement is not legaly binding. We are not responsible if your shanagans end up getting your breakfast posesed as well.)

Here is the original recipe for Together Breakfast created by the Babish Culinary Universe over on youtube.

Ingredients

Maple Caramel Corn Ingredients

  • 1/3C popcorn Kernels
  • 3Tbsp Coconut Oil
  • 1/2C Maple Syrup
  • 2Tbsp Water
  • 6Tbsp Unsalted Butter
  • 1/3C Brown Sugar, Packed
  • 1/2tsp Kosher Salt
  • 1/4tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1/8tsp Baking Soda

Yeasted Waffles Ingredients

  • 240g All-Purpose Flour
  • 1Tbsp Cornmeal
  • 1Tbsp Sugar
  • 1tsp Kosher Salt
  • 2tsp Instant Dry Yeast
  • 2 Eggs, Room Temperature
  • 1tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 480g Whole Milk
  • 113g (1 Stick) Unsalted Butter

Whipped Cream Ingredients

  • 8oz Heavy Cream, Cold
  • 2Tbsp Powdered Sugar
  • 1/2tsp Kosher Salt
  • 1tsp Vanilla Extract

Gourmet Together Breakfast Ingredients

  • 4 Yeasted Waffles
  • As needed good quality maple syrup
  • As needed caramel corn
  • As needed fresh whipped cream
  • 1 fresh strawberry
